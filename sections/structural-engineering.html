Structural Engineering

Monster D is a chamber thriller with a naturally strong structure once the antagonist enters the narrative. However, the film is currently front-loaded with softness and back-loaded with meaning, which inverts the natural balance of a psychological thriller. This analysis examines what the structure currently is, what it needs to be, and how to rebuild it with precision.

Structural Identity
Intended Identity: A single-location psychological thriller with escalating control dynamics, culminating in a violent but emotionally motivated climax.
