Vikram's Psychological Profile
Trauma & Mental State
Primary Trauma Sources
  • Addiction trauma: Alcoholism destroyed his family
  • Shame trauma: Witnessed by his son during drunk episodes
  • Loss trauma: Lost wife, son, job, identity
  • Recovery trauma: Fear of relapse haunts every moment
Current Mental State
Vikram is 1 year 8 months sober but psychologically fragile. He exhibits:
  • Hypervigilance around alcohol
  • Self-punishment tendencies
  • Emotional suppression
  • Deep-seated guilt about past
Psychological Authenticity
What Works Psychologically
  • Addiction response: His terror when forced to drink alcohol feels authentic
  • Shame behavior: Avoidance of eye contact with Isha tracks perfectly
  • Parental guilt: Desperation to protect Akhil rings true
  • Survival instinct: Fight-or-flight responses are believable
  • Emotional breakdown: Gradual unraveling under pressure is realistic
Psychological Gaps
  • Bar fight trigger unclear: What specifically caused the violence?
  • Early passivity unexplained: Why doesn't he fight harder initially?
  • Recovery process absent: How did he achieve 1yr 8mo sobriety?
  • PTSD symptoms underexplored: Would have flashbacks, hyperarousal

Psychological Rewrite Blueprint
Strategic recommendations for deepening psychological authenticity and behavioral consistency:
1. Establish Vikram's Emotional State Early
  • Add sobriety chip or AA token in his possession
  • Show him resisting drink at bar before fight
  • Include photo of Akhil that triggers bar fight
Creates psychological foundation from page 1
2. Unify Ranjit's Philosophical Core
Select one psychological worldview ("Power reveals identity") and ensure all his actions stem from this core belief system.
3. Add PTSD Markers for Vikram
  • Hypervigilance when officers appear
  • Flashback triggers (not full dreams)
  • Physical stress responses (trembling, sweating)
4. Justify Roopa's Trust
Show that she's new to the station, establishing why she wouldn't question authority. Add moment where she almost questions Ranjit but self-censors.
5. Clarify Isha's Decision Logic
Add beat where she receives official-sounding call (Ranjit manipulating) that makes bringing Akhil seem procedurally necessary.
6. Show Recovery Journey Traces
Brief mention of AA meetings, therapy, or support system that helped Vikram achieve sobriety. Makes his fear of relapse more poignant.
