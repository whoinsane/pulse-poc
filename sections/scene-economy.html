Scene Economy Principles
Essential Scene Functions
  • Plot Advancement: Move story forward
  • Character Revelation: Deepen understanding
  • Theme Building: Reinforce core ideas
  • Tension Escalation: Raise stakes/conflict
  • Emotional Resonance: Connect with audience
Rule: Every scene should serve at least 2-3 functions. Single-purpose scenes are candidates for cutting or merging.
Scene Economy Red Flags
  • Repetition: Same information conveyed multiple times
  • Treading water: No forward momentum
  • Unnecessary exposition: Info dumps without drama
  • Tonal breaks: Scenes that undermine atmosphere
  • Geographic confusion: Unclear spatial relationships
